Student Engagement Tracking — Option A Preview

Welcome desk forms — proposed redesign

This is a clickable preview of the proposed Microsoft Forms redesign for the welcome desk. It runs entirely in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere, and no Microsoft accounts are touched. Walk through both forms, submit a few entries, then check the submissions view to see how the data would land in Excel.

For volunteers

  • Student Engagement - Visitor Tracking  — log walk-ups at the desk by time block
  • Student Engagement - Outreach Tracking  — log a campus event or campaign

For Shannon

  • Live dashboard  — KPIs, heatmap, charts, filters by date / campus / time block (preview of what the Excel dashboard will look like)
  • View submissions & export CSV  — raw data table, download as CSV, pivot in Excel

What's different from the current forms

  • The 0–10 cap is gone. Counts are real integers — a busy day can finally show 47 wayfinding inquiries.
  • Empty category counts mean zero. Volunteers skip past what didn't come up; in Excel, blanks are treated as 0 by SUM.
  • Time block (Morning / Afternoon / Evening) is captured on the visitor form for peak-hour analysis.
  • Submitter Name & Email are captured automatically from the volunteer's Conestoga login (built-in Forms behavior — no extra question needed).
  • Outreach Activity is multi-select. A Pride Month + CCR table is one entry, not two.
  • The 24 visitor categories are grouped into 7 sections, with the most-frequent inquiries (Wayfinding, OneCard, IT Support, Bus Pass, Parking, Timetable) in the first section so volunteers don't have to scroll on a quiet shift.
Demo note: data is stored in your browser's local storage only. Clearing browser data, switching browsers, or opening this in a private window will reset the preview. Click Load sample data on the submissions page for a populated example.
